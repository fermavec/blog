Research & Analysis

With over 10 years of experience across analytics, business intelligence, system studies, communication and technology, my work focuses on Human-Centered Systems, Accessibility, Responsible AI and the socio-technical impacts of intelligent systems.

I examine AI-related phenomena through technical, cognitive, social and philosophical perspectives, translating complex developments into clear analysis and actionable insights.

As a visually impaired analyst, I maintain a particular interest in accessibility, multimodal interaction and inclusive design, exploring how intelligent systems can better align with human diversity and real-world needs.
