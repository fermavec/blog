Engineering & Analysis

I have spent over 10 years developing data systems and artificial intelligence, combining technical rigor with a deep analysis of their impact.

My professional approach integrates software engineering, data science, and behavioral analysis. I am focused on understanding how artificial systems process information and interact with human cognition.

As a visually impaired professional working extensively with digital systems, I am particularly interested in accessibility, multimodal interaction and the development of AI systems aligned with real human needs and cognitive diversity.

As an AI Analyst and Researcher, my work is divided into two main areas:

Technical Development: I build data architectures, AI systems, and human-centered applications. I implement robust solutions in Python and JavaScript, prioritizing accessibility, performance, and practical utility.

Research & Synthesis: I analyze AI developments and communicate complex technical systems with clarity. My goal is to translate the complexity of the state-of-the-art into actionable strategic insights, free of noise and unfounded expectations.

Understanding the architecture and mechanics of artificial systems is fundamental to integrating them strategically, maintaining clarity about their real capabilities and limitations.
